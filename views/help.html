Commonly Asked Questions

How do I send a message?

Open the side menu, navigate to 'Chat'. You will be prompted to enter a username, enter a username and select 'Ok'. Navigate to the text bar at the bottom of the display and enter your message. Then click the 'Send' button or press enter on your keyboard.

How do I send a file for someone to download?

Open the side menu, navigate to 'Chat'. You will be prompted to enter a username, enter a username and select 'Ok'. Navigate to the text bar at the bottom of the display and locate the paperclip symbol to the left. Click on the paperclip and select a file from your computer. The file will automatically send once 'Open' has been selected.

To download the file, simply click on the underlined file in the messaging area, the file will then download onto your computer.

How can I change my chat username?

If you want to change your username, open the side menu and select 'Chat', you will then be prompted to enter a new username and will rejoin the chat with this name.

How can I see who is in the chat with me?

In the chat, navigate to the top right where the 'Current user' and 'Users in chat' are located. Click on this button and a list of all current users will pop up. You can close this list by clicking on the 'X'.

How do I turn on dark mode?

Open the side menu, navigate to 'Theme' and click on the 'Toggle dark mode' button. This will turn dark mode on or off depending on your current theme and will remember this setting for next time.
